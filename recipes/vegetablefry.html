Quinoa & Vegetable Stir-Fry

Enjoy sweet, savory goodness with our Hawaiian-inspired Chicken & Vegetable Stir-Fry with Rice and Quinoa, ready in just 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions (for garnish)

How to make it

  1. Rinse quinoa thoroughly and cook with water or broth until fluffy (about 15 minutes).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and saut√© for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add bell pepper, zucchini, and broccoli; stir-fry for 5 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Stir in cooked quinoa, soy sauce, and sesame oil; mix well.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy your nutritious meal.
