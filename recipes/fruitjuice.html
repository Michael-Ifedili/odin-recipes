Citrus-ginger fruit juice

Citrus-ginger fruit juice is a vibrant, zesty beverage known for its refreshing taste and immune-boosting properties due to its high vitamin C and antioxidant content. It is highly customizable and can be prepared using a variety of citrus fruits and a blender or juicer.

Ingredients

  • 4 oranges, peeled
  • 1 grapefruit, peeled
  • 1 lemon, peeled
  • 1-inch piece fresh ginger
  • 1 small apple (for sweetness)
  • 6 fresh mint leaves (optional)
  • Cold water or ice (optional, to dilute)

Steps

  1. Wash all produce thoroughly and peel the citrus fruits.
  2. Core the apple and slice the ginger into smaller pieces.
  3. Juicer method: Feed ginger, apple, and citrus through the juicer, then add mint last.
  4. Blender method: Add all ingredients with 1/2 cup cold water; blend until smooth, then strain through a fine sieve or nut milk bag.
  5. Taste and adjust: add more lemon for brightness, apple for sweetness, or a few ice cubes to chill.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the fresh, zesty kick.
